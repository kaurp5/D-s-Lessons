Be kind to yourself

There will be days when you expect too much from yourself. You will think you should already know the answer, be stronger, move faster, or never make mistakes. On those days, I want you to remember something simple: you are allowed to be learning.

Speak to yourself with the same softness you would offer someone you love. Growth does not arrive through harshness. It grows best where there is patience, honesty, and grace.

Be brave, yes. Be accountable, yes. But also be gentle. The voice you build inside yourself will stay with you for a very long time. Make it a kind one.
